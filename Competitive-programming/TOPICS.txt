-HEAPS:
- is a tree datastructure where the parent is always bigger than children.

- Benefitial of heaps is to turn a tree into a list.
- We can go the other way around by turring a list into a heap

std::make_heap(begin(numbers), end(numbers));

- we can push a number:

numbers.push_back(8.88);
std:push_heap(begin(numbers), end(numbers));

- To remove the maximum value in a heap we use:

std::pop_heap(begin(numbers), end(numers));
